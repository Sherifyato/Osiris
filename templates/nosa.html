Exoplanet HD 209458 b 'Osiris'

HD 209458 b, also known as 'Osiris,' is a Jupiter-sized exoplanet orbiting a sun-like star. Discovered in 1999, it is known for its significant atmospheric studies. Here you can find graphical analysis and 3D visualizations of the exoplanet Osiris.
